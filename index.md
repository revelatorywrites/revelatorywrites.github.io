My name’s Mike Powell. I’m the author of a novel called _New Paltz, New Paltz_, which was published by [Double Negative](https://double--negative.com/001-new-paltz-new-paltz.html) in June 2025, and named a staff favorite by both the [Paris Review](https://www.theparisreview.org/blog/2025/12/12/our-favorite-books-of-2025/) and the [Los Angeles Review of Books](https://lareviewofbooks.org/article/best-book-2025-favorite-larb/), the latter of which did a nice [interview](https://lareviewofbooks.org/article/perfect-or-whatever/) with me. 

The book can be purchased through [Asterism](https://asterismbooks.com/product/new-paltz-new-paltz-mike-powell).
